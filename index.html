hello !

I'm Mattéo, a student sound designer based in France, currently finishing a master’s degree in sound design at the national school of games and interactive media (Conservatoire National des Arts et Métiers - Ecole Nationale du Jeu et Médias Itéractifs Numériques).

After some years of experience as audio freelancer on advertising, films and documentaries working as recordist, on set recordist, and mixer for all kinds of linear audiovisual projects, including on multichannel and Atmos formats, I decided to pursue sound design and game audio as my full-time occupation.
I'm deeply passionate about collaborating with a team of talented individuals, and i'm always aiming to put audio interactivity at the center of the experiences I work on.

This site showcases projects that I consider to be of interest if you wish to know more about my work. You'll find game audio projects as well as experiments in various sound-related fields. Additionally, you’ll find an archive of older, mostly linear audio projects I've worked on over the years.
